Welcome to the **unofficial** digital portfolio showcase dedicated to **Sabaa Al-Qaisi (سبأ القيسي)** — an Erbil-based visual artist and interior designer transforming rigid architectural spaces into vibrant living environments.

---

## 🎨 About the Artist

Drawing and painting since childhood, Saba built her multidisciplinary creative career through self-taught perseverance. Originally coming from a background in agricultural sciences, her lifelong passion for colors and spatial harmony led her to master interior wall murals, fluid acrylic pours on stretched canvas, and high-gloss epoxy resin installations.

Operating from her studio in **Erbil**, Saba’s creative philosophy centers on breathing emotional warmth and organic vitality into everyday living and formal spaces. Her artwork has been featured in multiple regional fine art exhibitions and cultural galleries across Iraq.

---

## 🌟 Artistic Specialties

* **Interior Wall Murals**: Bespoke full-wall fluid acrylic pours and metallic accent walls tailored to residential and luxury lofts.
* **Fluid Canvas Art**: Explorations of organic lacing, silicone cell formations, and energetic color symphonies on stretched canvas.
* **High-Gloss Resin**: Deep-gloss epoxy resin artwork harmonizing vibrant turquoise and magenta pigments with gold foil accents.
* **Spatial Consultations**: Collaborative interior styling consultations designed to give your home a unique emotional heartbeat.
