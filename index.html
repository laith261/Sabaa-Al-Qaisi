Hello! I’m Sabaa Al-Qaisi (سبأ القيسي). Drawing and painting since childhood, my creative journey bridges the gap between structured interior architecture and expressive visual spontaneity.

Coming from a background in agricultural sciences, my lifelong creative passion led me to build a multidisciplinary career in interior design and fluid arts through self-taught perseverance. Today, based in Erbil, I specialize in breathing warmth and emotional resonance into rigid spaces.

Whether painting fluid pours on canvas, crafting high-gloss epoxy resin, or commissioning full-wall architectural murals, my promise is simple: we will transform your home into an authentic reflection of your personal joy.
